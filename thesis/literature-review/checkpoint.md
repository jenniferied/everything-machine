## Key Findings

### What is Artistic Research?

Based on Phase 1 reading:

1. **Frayling's Trichotomy (1993):**
   - Research INTO art (historical/theoretical)
   - Research THROUGH art (practice-led, materials-based)
   - Research FOR art (reference gathering)
   - → My project: primarily "through" with elements of all three

2. **Borgdorff's Position (2012):**
   - Research IN/THROUGH art produces knowledge embodied in artistic outcomes
   - Artistic practice as epistemological site
   - → Legitimizes website + journal as research outputs

3. **Henke Manifesto (2019):**
   - Warns against uncritical adoption of academic methods
   - "Aesthetic knowledge" as distinct from discursive knowledge
   - Singularity, showing, indeterminacy as research modes
   - → Supports experimental, non-linear approach

4. **Schön's Reflection-in-Action (1983):**
   - Tacit knowledge in professional practice
   - "Reflective conversation with the situation"
   - → Core method for documenting KI workflow

5. **Wesseling's "Experimental Foothold" (2017):**
   - Constraints create intellectual freedom
   - Personal urgency → systematic reflection → objective contribution
   - → Guides research question development
